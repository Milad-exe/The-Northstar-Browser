Rules

  • Colors come from tokens only — never hex in markup.
  • Square corners everywhere. No drop shadows; hierarchy comes from borders and surface steps.
  • Micro labels are mono + uppercase + tracked; body copy is Manrope.
  • Hover states use bg-hover, active states bg-active.
